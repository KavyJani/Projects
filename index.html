Marketing Analysis On Netflix:

Netflix Data:

Data Requirement:

Subscriber count, revenue, and marketing spend data by region (Asia-Pacific, EMEA, Latin America, US & Canada) from Q1 2018 to Q2 2020, including metrics to calculate customer acquisition cost (CAC) and ARPU.

Data Summary:

Between Q1 2018 and Q2 2020, Asia-Pacific showed the highest growth in subscribers and revenue. Other regions displayed varied trends, with a clear link between lower CAC and stronger profitability across all regions.

Analysis:

I analyzed regional trends in subscriber growth, revenue performance, and CAC using Excel functions like IFERROR, growth rate calculations, and pivot tables. Data cleaning ensured accuracy while visualizations revealed performance disparities and marketing efficiency across the four major global regions.

Solutions:

Recommendations included optimizing CAC in the US & Canada, boosting ARPU in Latin America, investing in Asia-Pacific, and reducing churn in EMEA. These strategic actions aim to enhance profitability, improve regional performance, and sustain long-term subscriber and revenue growth globally.

Perfomance Analysis:

Perfomance Analysis:

I cleaned raw data using Excel by removing duplicates, formatting numbers, and standardising fields. In Power BI, I imported the cleaned data, created relationships, and used DAX functions like SUM(), AVERAGE(), DIVIDE(), and CALCULATE() to compute Total Revenue, Total Spends, ROI, and Growth metrics. Finally, I designed interactive dashboards with clustered charts, pie charts, and KPI cards to visualize regional and campaign performance. Data Summary: The dashboard shows a total revenue of 42.54M and total spends of 25.69M, achieving an average ROI of 0.68. Among regions, Africa generated the highest revenue and spend, followed by Asia and North America. Influencer Marketing emerged as the best-performing campaign with the highest ROI (1.10) and total ROI (137), whereas TV Commercials had the lowest ROI (0.45). Digital campaigns made up 62.5% of total campaigns, while traditional campaigns accounted for 37.5%. Overall, the total ROI across campaigns was 676.25.

Recommendations:

●Increase investment in Influencer Marketing and Social Media Ads to maximize ROI.
●Reassess or reduce spending on TV Commercials and Email Marketing due to lower ROI performance.
●Expand Digital campaigns, given their higher proportion and success rate compared to traditional campaigns.
●Focus future regional strategies on Africa, Asia, and North America, where spend-to-revenue conversion is stronger.

Credit Card Analysis :

Data Summary :

I conducted a comprehensive Credit Card Analysis project to uncover patterns in customer behavior, transaction performance, and delinquency. Using SQL, I cleaned and segmented data across card types, demographics, job profiles, and expenditure categories. I built interactive Power BI dashboards that visualized revenue, total interest, transaction count, and income by quarter, usage method, and customer segments. Key insights showed that Blue cards and businessmen drove the highest revenue, while retirees and low-income groups had elevated delinquency. These findings supported targeted strategy recommendations to reduce credit risk and enhance revenue.

Recommendations:

●Target High-Performing Segments: Focus marketing and rewards programs on Blue cardholders and businessmen, who contribute the highest revenue.
●Mitigate Delinquency Risk: Implement stricter credit controls or tailored payment plans for retirees and low-income customers, who show higher delinquency.
●Optimize Usage Methods: Promote swipe transactions over online methods, as they generate the highest revenue.
●Segmented Credit Offers: Design personalized credit products for self-employed and graduate segments, who show strong income and usage potential.
